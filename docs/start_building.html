4.6 ğŸ‘· Hire the Construction Worker on the Land (Add publish.yml)

The last step is to hire a construction worker that lives on your land.

This worker automatically rebuilds your house whenever new materials arrive by truck.

This worker is a small file called: .github/workflows/publish.yml
